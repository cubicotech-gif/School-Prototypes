Our Programs

From first steps to confident scholars.

Three programs, one continuous journey. Each one age-appropriate, play-rich at younger ages, and academically rigorous where it counts.

Ages 3 – 5
Early Years

Phonics, numbers, art, and music — taught through purposeful, joyful play that builds the foundation for lifelong learning.

  • Synthetic phonics
  • Early numeracy
  • Art, music & movement
  • Social-emotional learning
Ages 5 – 11
Primary

A structured academic program with strong reading, writing, mathematics, and inquiry-based science and social studies.

  • English, maths, science
  • Urdu & Islamiat
  • Library culture
  • Sports & PE daily
Ages 11+
Secondary

Rigorous preparation for board examinations, combined with critical thinking, character education, and leadership opportunities.

  • Board exam preparation
  • Critical thinking workshops
  • Leadership programs
  • Career counselling
Our Approach

What 40 years of teaching has taught us.

A few principles that quietly shape every Playway classroom — from Early Years to Secondary.

Standards without fear

We hold high expectations and we pair them with patient teaching. Children rise to standards when they don't feel afraid to fall short.

Reading is sacred

A child who reads well can learn anything. Reading and library time are protected hours at Playway, not extras.

Small classes, real attention

Our 15:1 ratio isn't a marketing line — it's the reason every teacher knows every child by name and by need.

Character on the timetable

Honesty, perseverance, kindness — taught through assembly, mentorship, and the everyday moments that shape a child.
