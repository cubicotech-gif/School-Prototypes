Our Programs

A thoughtful path from first steps to confident scholars.

Every program is age-appropriate, play-rich at younger ages, and academically rigorous where it counts.

Ages 3 – 5
Early Years

Phonics, numbers, art, and music — all taught through purposeful, joyful play that builds the foundation for lifelong learning.

Ages 5 – 11
Primary

A structured academic program with strong reading, writing, mathematics, and inquiry-based science and social studies.

Ages 11+
Secondary

Rigorous preparation for board examinations, combined with critical thinking, character education, and leadership opportunities.
