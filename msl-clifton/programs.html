Our Programs

A learning journey designed for every stage.

From a child's first hesitant morning at Playgroup to confident young readers in Primary, every MSL program is built around what children actually need at that age.

Ages 2 – 3
Playgroup

A gentle introduction to school life through guided play, sensory activities, and warm one-on-one care.

  • Half-day mornings
  • Sensory & messy play
  • Story time & rhymes
  • Settling-in period for parents
Ages 3 – 4
Nursery

Building social skills, early literacy, and creative expression in a joyful, structured environment.

  • Pre-phonics & mark making
  • Number sense through play
  • Art, music & movement
  • Group circle time daily
Ages 4 – 6
KG I & II

Foundational reading, writing, and numeracy paired with art, music, and outdoor exploration.

  • Synthetic phonics
  • Early writing skills
  • Hands-on maths
  • Outdoor learning
Ages 6 – 11
Primary

A rigorous yet nurturing curriculum that builds confident, curious learners ready for the next stage.

  • English, maths, science
  • Urdu & Islamiat
  • Art, music, PE
  • Library & reading culture
Our Approach

What every MSL day actually looks like.

Our curriculum isn't a list of subjects on paper. It's a rhythm of learning that respects how children grow.

Play with purpose

Every game, every craft, every story has a reason — building motor skills, vocabulary, social awareness, or curiosity.

Small group learning

Most lessons happen in groups of 6 – 8, so every child gets attention and no question goes unanswered.

Daily outdoor time

Our covered playground gives children a chance to run, climb, and breathe — every day, regardless of the weather.

Parent partnership

Regular updates, open-door conversations, and termly meetings keep parents in the loop on what their child is learning.
