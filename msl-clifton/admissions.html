Why Families Choose MSL

The school that puts your child first.

Choosing your child's first school is one of the biggest decisions a parent makes. We don't take that lightly. From the moment you reach out, our team is here to answer every question — no pressure, no rushing.

When you visit, you'll meet the teachers your child would actually have. You'll see the classrooms in action. You'll get a feel for the daily rhythm. And when you're ready, we'll walk you through the simple application together.

How to Apply

Four simple steps, one warm welcome.

No exams, no entry tests for younger children, no surprises. Here's how it works.

01

Get in touch

Call, message, or fill out the contact form. We respond within one working day.

02

Visit campus

We'll schedule a 30-minute visit so you can meet the teachers and see the classrooms.

03

Submit application

Fill out a short form and bring your child's documents. Our team will guide you through it.

04

Welcome to MSL

Once accepted, we plan a gentle settling-in period so your child starts school feeling confident.
